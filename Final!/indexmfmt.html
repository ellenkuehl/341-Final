Friends of Mineral Town and More Friends of Mineral Town are almost identical to the Back To Nature series. There are, however, some prominent differences in this handheld series!

  • The graphics in MFOMT are 2D, BTN has 3D graphics.
  • Mines in MFOMT have 255 floors instead of 10. (This is excluding the winter mine in BTN.)
  • Gift-giving is more complex in MFOMT.
  • There is no hothouse expansion in MFOMT.
  • There are different festivals between the games.
  • Can connect MFOMT to gamecube and share information with the Another Wonderful Life game.
  • Animals gain affection when left outdoors in MFOMT.
  • You can saver your game anytime, anywhere in MFOMT!
