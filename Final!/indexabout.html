From the beginning...

Harvest Moon was developed by Japanese artist Yasuhiro Wada who grew up on a farm outside of Tokyo. When Wada moved to Tokyo to further his career, he missed the country and noticed many values and elements of the countryside that deserved appreciation. This inspired his idea for Harvest Moon. More on Wada here!

Now, Harvest Moon is considered a Japanese legend, and one of the most popular games in Japanese history! Most Harvest Moon games have the same introduction and storyline-the main character's grandfather dies, so they acquire the farm and are challenged to bring the land back to life!

This site is about celebrating Harvest Moon, discussing the differences between male and female game versions, as well as sharing tips and secrets of the HM series. I have decided to begin with some of my favorite games, and will continue to add as I go! For more information on Harvest Moon, I have embeded two videos both talking about Harvest Moon's creational history. The video on the left is about 25 minutes, the one on the right is about 7 minutes. Everyone has time to learn about Harvest Moon! Please enjoy!
